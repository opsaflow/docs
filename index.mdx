## Why Rivestack

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="pgvector built-in" icon="brain">
    pgvector is enabled by default on your initial database. Build RAG, semantic search, and recommendation systems without extra setup.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automatic failover" icon="shield-halved">
    Patroni-managed HA clusters with streaming replication. Failover happens in seconds with zero data loss.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Daily backups" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    Automated daily backups with 14-day retention. Point-in-time recovery to any second.
  </Card>
</Columns>
